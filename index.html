Time to talk about one of the few cat-ownership controversies: Indoor or outdoor? We'll get down to the nitty-gritty, and the myths relating to cat enrichment with the outside world.
Some Facts About Cats

Cats are so intelligent that they're constantly seeking ways to entertain themselves. Let them be involved in your daily, whether that's cooking or folding laundry!

Cats prefer fresh clean water daily much like we do. It's important to keep them hydrated to avoid health problems in the future.

All cats enjoy companionship, but generally do better when introduced as a kitten. Cat, or dog, they make great friends for existing pets, too.

The number one thing your cat wants from you, is your affection. They love their people!
